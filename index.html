How To Play Chess

How to Play this Chess:

Leftclick on one of your figures to select it.
Another leftclick on a yellow square to move or on one other piece that you own, to select the figure.

Move which this game is not capable yet: Rochade, En passant and switch Pawn to queen. See Github issues for a full discription.
